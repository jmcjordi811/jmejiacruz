Welcome! This website includes information about Asymptotic Notations for CS students.

Asymptotic Notations are three different mathimatical notations that help us analyze an
algorithm's running time which allow programmers to make their programs faster.

These three notations are called:

  • Big Theta
  • Big O
  • Big Omega


Click on the three different menu links to learn a little bit more about these notations.
